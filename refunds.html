1. Digital products

Our products are digital goods and services (such as online courses and software). Because they are delivered electronically and accessed immediately, specific rules apply to refunds, as set out below.

2. EU right of withdrawal (14-day cooling-off period)

As a consumer in the EU, you normally have the right to withdraw from a purchase within 14 days without giving a reason. For digital content, this right applies unless you have agreed that we may start delivering it immediately and you have acknowledged that you thereby lose your right of withdrawal (see section 3).

3. Consent & loss of the right of withdrawal

When you purchase digital content that is made available immediately, you are asked to expressly consent to immediate access and to acknowledge that, once access begins, you lose your 14-day right of withdrawal for that content. Where you have not yet accessed the content, you may still request a refund within the 14-day period. Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights. If a product is faulty, does not work as described, or you cannot access what you paid for, you are entitled to a repair, replacement, or refund as required by law. Please contact us and we will put it right.
